### Typography
```
Display: Cormorant Garamond
  - Light (300) for elegance
  - Regular (400) for body
  - Medium (500) for emphasis
  - Semibold (600) for headers
  - Letter spacing: -0.02em
  - Line height: 1.05-1.2

Body: Inter
  - Light (300) for captions
  - Regular (400) for body
  - Medium (500) for buttons
  - Semibold (600) for labels
```
