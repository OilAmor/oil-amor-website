## PRICING FORMULAS

### Pure Essential Oil (New Bottle)
```
Cost = (Volume × Oil$/ml) + $4.00 + (Chips × $0.25) + $5.00
Retail = Cost ÷ 0.51, round to .95
```

### Carrier Blend (New Bottle)
```
Cost = (OilVolume × Oil$/ml) + (CarrierVolume × $0.083) + $4.00 + (Chips × $0.25) + $6.00
Retail = Cost ÷ 0.50, round to .95
```

### Pure Refill
```
Cost = (Volume × Oil$/ml) + $6.00 + (Chips × $0.25) + $2.50
50ml: Retail = Cost ÷ 0.55, round to .95
100ml: Retail = Cost ÷ 0.60, round to .95
```

### Carrier Refill
```
Cost = (OilVolume × Oil$/ml) + (CarrierVolume × $0.083) + $6.00 + $2.50
50ml: Retail = Cost ÷ 0.55, round to .95
100ml: Retail = Cost ÷ 0.60, round to .95
```
